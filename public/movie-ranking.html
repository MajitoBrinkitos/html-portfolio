The Best Movies By Year

All-time Top 3 Movies

By Maria-Jose MC

West Side Story

From 1961 and directed by Jerome Robbins and Robert Wise, tells the story a young woman and man that fall in love, despite the social opposition from their families and friends.

101 Dalmatians

From 1961 and directed by Clyde Geronimi, Hamilton Luske, and Wolfgang, this is the original story of Pongo and Perdita, two Dalmatians that fall in love, and join forces when their puppies are stolen by Cruella de Ville.

Under Paris

From 2024 by Xavier Gens, this film is sharks vs police when a gigiantic shark threats the city of Paris.
